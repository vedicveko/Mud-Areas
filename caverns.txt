*Caverns of the Pale Man
*Written by Kaan for Dibrova

Level: 25 - 50
100 rooms
23 mobs
45 objs
Connector Zones: The Plains of Blood

These are a sytem of caverns made of black rock which catacomb for
many miles. There are a number of cavernous mobs, all of them
relatively high level - even the bats kick some ass. This zone is 
mainly intended to be a zone for high level players to gain good exp
and find some good eq.
