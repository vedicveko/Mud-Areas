The original concept of this zone was 'The Tower of Nirvana' which
consisted of some 30-40 rooms, all set within a tower and the immediate
perimeter. The plane area around it was added later with the majority
of the mobs and objs. Very fun mobs all with decent eq. A players'
favorite.
