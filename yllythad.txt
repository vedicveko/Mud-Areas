*Yllythad Sea
*Written by Kaan for Dibrova

Level: 10 - 40
300 rooms
15 mobs
9 objs
Connector Zones: Beach and Lighthouse, McGintey Bay

This is a fairly large sea made strictly for the purpose of connecting
new continents and islands to the MUD. The mobs have little eq as most
of them are fish or other sea-faring animals. This zone can add a
great deal of openings for builders to add to the overall theme of the
MUD.
