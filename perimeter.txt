*Eastern Army Perimeter
*Written by Kaan for Dibrova

Level: 10 - 30
80 rooms
21 mobs
33 objs
Connector Zones: Eastern Army Encampment, Light Forest

This is the outer boundary of the Eastern Army, the governmental
military operation for Dibrova. There are some spec_procs which are
used on Dibrova - these may be found on the Coder's Page. This is
a good experience zone for mid-level players plus a minimum amount
of decent eq can be had.
